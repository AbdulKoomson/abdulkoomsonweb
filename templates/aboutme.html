About Me: Data Science & AI Specialist, Data Engineer, Full-Stack Developer, Builder

I’m a data-driven problem solver with a strong foundation in statistical modeling, data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. My work blends deep technical knowledge with practical applications in web development, AI product design, and data engineering.

With both a Master’s and Bachelor’s degree in Data Science and Analytics—earned with honors and President’s List recognition from Thomas Edison State University—I bring academic depth to every challenge I tackle. My journey actually began in Civil Engineering, where I first learned to break down complex problems structurally. Today, I do the same using Python, data pipelines, and machine learning.

🧠 My Technical Focus

I specialize in the PyData ecosystem and leverage tools like:

  • Python, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn
  • PyTorch, PyMC3 for deep learning and probabilistic models
  • Apache Spark & Databricks for big data processing
  • Azure for cloud computing, storage, and deployment

I’ve built and deployed intelligent systems that:

  • Transform raw data into decision-ready dashboards
  • Deploy real-time AI solutions via scalable backend services
  • Engineer data pipelines for acquisition, processing, and delivery
  • Create dynamic UIs that simplify insight consumption
  • Manage cloud infrastructure, version control, and team workflows

📜 Certifications

  • Microsoft Certified: Azure Data Engineer Associate
  • ESRI Spatial Data Science Certification
  • Tableau Specialist – Interactive Visualization

👨‍👩‍👧‍👧 Life Outside of Code

I’m a husband and father to two amazing daughters. Outside of work, I’m a dedicated gym-goer (even on the days I don’t feel like it), a sports enthusiast, and a big believer in structure, planning, and continuous improvement.

Whether it’s time-blocking my calendar or staying up late chasing a new idea, I’m intentional about balancing health, family, work, and my startup ambitions. I believe in making room for the things that matter—with no excuses.

🧠 What Drives Me

I love Python. I love solving problems. And I love working in environments where the pressure is real and the pace is fast. I'm constantly tinkering with new startup ideas—even if most never leave the notebook. The ones that do? I commit to them deeply, building them as seriously as I would any paid product.

That mindset shapes how I build this startup—one blog post, experiment, and prototype at a time.

If you’re building in the AI space, exploring career paths in data science, or just curious about my stack and story, reach out. I’m always up for a real convo.
